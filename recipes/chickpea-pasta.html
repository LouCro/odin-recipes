Ingredients

  • 1 x Can of Chickpeas
  • 4x Large Garlic Cloves
  • Pinch of Pepper Flakes
  • 1/2 Cup Parm
  • Salt
  • Pasta
  • Lemon
  • Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Drain chickpeas and place in a saucepan over medium heat with two tbspâ€™s olive oil. Chop and add garlic to the pan, add rosemary and chilli.
  2. Put pasta onto boil.
  3. Once chickpeas are crispy, remove half a cup and place in a bowl. Continue to cook the remaining chickpeas in the pan, adding pasta water and mashing to transform into a pulpy consistency.
  4. Place cooked pasta in the pan with chickpeas. Add parmesan, salt and lemon zest. Stir in a tbsp of butter.
  5. Serve with a squeeze of lemon, parmesan and the left-over chickpeas.

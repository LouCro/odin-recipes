Ingredients

  • 6x Spring Onions
  • 1x Leek
  • 1x Large Broccoli (broccolini)
  • Olive oil
  • 2x Garlic Cloves
  • 100g Spinach
  • Â½ Cup of frozen Peas
  • Pasta (shells are the best!)
  • 50000g Parmesan Cheese (like wtf its literally da flavour)
  • Coriander (if you hate yourself)

Steps

  1. Trim and slice the spring onions. Trim the leek, halve it lengthways, wash it, then finely slice it. Finally, slice the broccoli stalks, reserving the tips to the side.
  2. Put a large non-stick shallow pan on medium heat.
  3. Put 1tbsp of oil into the pan, with the spring onions, leeks and broccoli stalks. Peel and finely chop the garlic and add to the pan
  4. Add the spinach and peas, then add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for 5 to 10 minutes, or until soft.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in a large pan of boiling salted water, adding the broccoli florets in the last 2 minutes
  7. Scoop 200mls of cooking water from the pasta pan and add it to the veggies. Now, blitz the mixture until it is smooth.
  8. Drain the pasta and broccoli and tip into the sauce with the parmesan.
